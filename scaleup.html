STARTUP TO SCALE UP

About Global Acceleration Program

StartupToScaleup is supported by Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India (GOD that predominately aims to elevate the startups in the Healthcare and Edtech sectors to the international markets.

StartupToScaleup Acceleration Program

  • 6 weeks of India Program
  • 4 weeks of Global Immersion Program
  • Market Entry Strategies, Business modal review and Partnerships
  • No equity dilution
  • Support services such as co-working space, CXO services and legal & accounting support
  • Investor and mentor connections
